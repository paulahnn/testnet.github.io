Eliminating food deserts

KitcheNet prepares tasty, affordable, healthy, ready-to-eat family meals using fresh ingredients and delivers them to spots integrated into their daily routine

Learn More

Our Mission

In Chicago, it is estimated that 600,000 people live in food deserts, more than a mile from fresh groceries. Parents usually do not cook at home due to lack of time and depend heavily on processed cans and fast food chains because of their low price and high brand-awareness. Children, nearly one-third of these residents, then develop unhealthy eating habits and severe health problems such as obesity and diabetes at a very early age.

Our customer survey further shows a huge demand for healthy food for children among low and middle income families. KitcheNet saves parents’ time by bringing ready-to-eat, tasty, and healthy meals to sites they frequent, such as daycares and churches.

KitcheNet’s family meals are offered at the same low price as the fast food family meals and are developed with taste and dignity in mind to reinforce purchasing behavior and form healthy eating habits.

Our Impact

The KitcheNet team is dedicated to making our social impact more accessible and economical through our unique business model.

  • We rescue food excess to reduce food waste
  • We donate 100% of our excess to 0%
  • We hire and source locally
  • We’re SNAP* eligible

* Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program
